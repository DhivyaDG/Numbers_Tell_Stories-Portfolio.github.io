Books What I liked?  
HeadFirst Statistics
 I enjoy comics and never anticipated a technical book presented in comic style. This book is highly interactive and engaging which interests the readers to understand the concepts well.
Storytelling With Data
 I'm a big fan of colors, but after reading this book, I realized that if not used thoughtfully, colors can easily become cluttered and hinder understanding from the audience's perspective. This book highlights the importance of presenting data in a clear and engaging way. It teaches us how to use design principles to make data easier to understand and more impactful. The book also shows how to avoid common mistakes in data visualization and create visuals that connect with the audience.
Supercharge Power BI
 This book has helped me to understand and effecively use Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) to create better Power BI reports and dashboards
Fundamental Financial Accounting concepts
 Coming from an IT background, this book was instrumental in helping me grasp key financial concepts. Its clear explanations and lots of real-world examples simplified complex concepts, making them easy to understand and apply.
